C# Sharp

C#[b] (pronounced C sharp) is a general-purpose, multi-paradigm programming language encompassing strong typing, lexically scoped, imperative and component-oriented programming disciplines.It was developed around 2000 by Microsoft within its .NET initiative and later approved as a standard by Ecma (ECMA-334). C# was designed by Anders Hejlsberg.
